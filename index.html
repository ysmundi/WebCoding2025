FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How do I register?

Click "Register" and choose your role. Fill out the form and create your account.

How do I login?

Click "Login" on the homepage. Enter your email and password to access your dashboard.

Is JOBCONN free to use?

Yes, all core features are free. Recruiters pay only if they want to post more than the free limit.

How do I apply?

Log in and browse job listings. Click "Apply" and submit your application.

How do I post a position?

Log in to your employer account. Click "Post a Job," enter details, submit, and wait for admin approval.
