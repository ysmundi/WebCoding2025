PRIVACY POLICY

At JOB CONN, we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our users. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard the personal information of students and recruiters who use our platform.

We collect the following information:

Student

  • Full name, email address, and contact information
  • Educational background, including school, degree, and graduation year
  • Work experience, skills, and career interests
  • Resume or CV file

Recruiter

  • Company name, email address, and contact information
  • Job postings, including position details and qualifications
  • Recruiter notes and feedback on student applications

How We Use Your Information

  • To facilitate the connection between students and recruiters.
  • To match students with relevant job opportunities based on their profile.
  • To provide updates and notifications about the hiring process.
  • To improve our platform and services based on user feedback and usage data.
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

  • We will not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their own marketing purposes without your consent.
  • We may share your information with trusted service providers who assist us in operating the platform, such as hosting or data storage providers.
  • We may disclose your information if required by law or to protect the rights, property, or safety of our users or the public.

Data Security

  • We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.
  • We regularly review and update our security practices to ensure the ongoing protection of your data.

User Rights and Choices

  • You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information stored on our platform.
  • You can opt-out of receiving marketing communications from us at any time.
  • If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy practices, please contact our Privacy Officer at privacy@example.com.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

  • We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws.
  • We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy on our website or sending you an email.

By using our platform, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our practices, please do not use our services.
