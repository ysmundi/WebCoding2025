JOB FEST
10/20 - 10/25

The Job Fest is an exciting opportunity for students in the New York City area to connect directly with top recruiters from leading companies. Over the course of a week, students will have the chance to attend networking events, participate in mock interviews, and learn about the latest job opportunities in their fields of interest. Recruiters will be on-site to engage with students, review resumes, and conduct on-the-spot interviews, making this an invaluable chance for students to make meaningful connections and potentially secure internships or full-time roles. With a range of industries represented, from tech and finance to marketing and non-profit, the Job Fest promises to be a dynamic and career-boosting experience for all attendees.
